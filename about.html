About Wānaka FC

Discover the heart and soul of our football community

History

Developing Football in Otago

Founded in 2010, Wānaka FC is a thriving football club based in the Central Otago region of New Zealand. The club has over 550 members across Junior, Youth and Seniors. Our junior players participate in the Central Otago club season, our youth in the Southern Football Youth Development League, and our seniors play in the Southern League and Central Otago Football League.

For The Community

We take pride in being a local community club for everyone, catering to all ages, stages and abilities from the age of 5+ years. Wānaka FC also offers a development pathway for aspirational young players through The Academy.

Our Team

Thomas Van Hees

Director of Football

With a sport management degree, over 20 years of playing, and nearly 10 years of coaching experience, Thomas is a highly qualified professional in the New Zealand football landscape.


During his playing career in the Netherlands, Thomas had the privilege of playing with players who have played for over a decade in the Dutch Eredivisie and has been coached by UEFA A licensed coaches.


Inspired by his own coaches, he started paying it forward by coaching a junior football team at his local club in the Netherlands. 18 years after that first experience, Thomas has coached juniors & youth in New Zealand. In New Zealand Thomas has been involved with the Southern United Academy, Central Otago Academy, Olé Football Academy and now The Academy at Wānaka FC. Thomas has also worked as a Football Development Officer for Southern Football. Delivering Skill Centres and skill stations for the whole Central Otago region.


Thomas is proud to hold OFC/NZF accreditation as a community and advanced Coach Educator, further enhancing his ability to develop and mentor aspiring players and coaches. In addition, Thomas is OFC/NZF B-Licence accredited and looks forward to becoming an A-Licence coach in the future. His diverse coaching background and expertise make him a valuable asset to The Academy at Wānaka FC.

Josh Shackleton

Goalkeeper Coach

Josh is the goalkeeper coach for The Academy at Wānaka FC. Josh also plays in the Wānaka FC First Team as starting goalkeeper.


Originally from Auckland, Josh played for his boyhood club Papakura City FC from First Kicks at 5 years old, all the way through to the First Team.


Josh has gained great experience as a goalkeeper coach in an academy environment over the last couple of years, and he's highly regarded by the local youth academy players. Being a senior player, he also adds value as a leader and mentor for our young academy players as they make the transition into the First Team environment.


Not having access to goalkeeper-specific training as a young goalkeeper himself, Josh loves having the opportunity to offer this to aspiring goalkeepers now. He enjoys seeing players challenge themselves to get better, take on advice from training and put it into play.
